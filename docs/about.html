About Nakametrics

Nakametrics was created with a simple idea: that Bitcoin’s growing role in the financial system is one of the most important shifts in modern markets — and there’s still plenty of room to explore it through fresh charts, new ideas, and different perspectives.

This project focuses on visualizing companies that hold Bitcoin on their balance sheets, along with market behavior related to corporate Bitcoin exposure. The goal is to present these stories visually — in ways that stand apart from others in the space.

All charted data comes from publicly available sources and may occasionally be limited or delayed. Charts are updated regularly (ideally daily) to provide both short-term signals and long-term context.

Content includes:

  • Bitcoin holdings and balance sheet exposure among public companies
  • Market activity across bitcoin and related equities
  • Visual relationships between price, volume, and corporate treasury moves
  • Bitcoin accumulation trends
  • Yield estimates and valuation models for Bitcoin holdings
  • Bitcoin balance sheet exposure among public companies

The mission is to bring fresh insight and clarity to the Bitcoin-financial ecosystem through charts and visualizations.

About The Creator:

The site was created by, and is maintained by a visually-impaired bitcoin enthusiast (VIBE) whose interests include freedom money, Bitcoin, Bitcoin-related companies, data visualization, and data analysis. For some time, I have admired the exciting concepts others have created or build, after my a diagnosis with a progressive degenerative visual impairment in June 2024, I decided that delaying any concepts further may only result in them becoming more challenging in the future. The website and created charts are designed for a black background out of personal necessity due to my own visual impairment, which compromises my ability to see content with lots of white content. I apologize to those who have the opposite impairment.

NOTE: This site is unrelated to any company nor existing project and intends in no way to provide financial advice. Please consult documentation on official company websites and platforms for relevant financial information.
